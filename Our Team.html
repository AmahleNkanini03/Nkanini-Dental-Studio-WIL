Our Team

“Meet the passionate team behind Nkanini Dental Studio — professionals dedicated to brightening your smile and making your dental journey comfortable and stress-free.”

Dr Sandile
Nkanini

Meet the heart behind Nkanini Dental Studio. With a passion for making dentistry affordable and accessible, Dr. Nkanini founded the practice to serve communities in both Sterkspruit (Eastern Cape) and Reitz (Free State).

Specialising in both general and cosmetic dentistry, Dr. Nkanini combines professional expertise with a gentle, patient-focused approach. Beyond the clinic, Dr. Nkanini is dedicated to community outreach, school programs, and health awareness campaigns — believing that oral health is the foundation of confidence and overall wellbeing.

Sithembile Ngubane

Dental Assistant

She plays a key role in ensuring that every visit runs smoothly and comfortably. From preparing patients for their procedures to assisting the dentist chairside, Sithembile brings both skill and compassion to the practice.

Meme Voko

Receptionist

As the first friendly face you meet at Nkanini Dental Studio, Meme ensures that every patient feels welcomed from the moment they walk in. She manages appointments, patient records, and communication with care and professionalism.
